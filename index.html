Cranbourne East Primary School

On behalf of our school community I extend a very warm welcome to Cranbourne East Primary School. The school opened in 2011 and brings together contemporary features of a world class, education designed facility. We foster a vibrant culture of student-centred learning based on our co-teaching philosophy and through the creation of flexible, functional spaces.

Key features of the school include high expectations for all students, high quality teaching and innovative learning experiences. These features provide the strong foundations for life long learning and high levels of student engagement and student connectedness to learning is linked to a personalised and inquiry based learning approach. All learning spaces and programs promote active student engagement in a caring and well-resourced, environmentally sustainable setting highlighting high level parent and community involvement.
